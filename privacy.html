Privacy Policy

Zestine Technologies ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use ZeManage, how we use it, and the choices you have. By installing or using ZeManage you agree to the practices described below.

Effective: 25 May 2026 Version 1.0

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • The ZeManage plugin for Autodesk Revit (versions 2021–2026), including its installer, ribbon commands, and bundled addons (NWC Export, Link Remapper).
  • The associated cloud services that ZeManage communicates with, including the API at api.zemanage.com and the SignalR real-time hub.
  • The administrative web portal at zemanage.com (login, audit dashboards, OTP management).

It does not apply to Autodesk Revit itself, your file storage provider (BIM 360, ACC, local drives, on-premise file servers), or any third-party plugin you may install separately.

Authentication Tokens

  • Access tokens — held in memory only, never written to disk. Used to authorize API calls during a Revit session.
  • Refresh tokens — stored locally in %APPDATA%\BIManage\auth.dat, encrypted using Windows DPAPI (current-user scope). Used to renew access tokens without re-prompting for your password.
  • Cached identity — user ID, email, company, role flags, and profile ID stored locally in %APPDATA%\BIManage\identity.dat, encrypted using Windows DPAPI.

DPAPI encryption means these files can only be read by your Windows user account on the same device. Logging out or uninstalling deletes them.

How We Use Your Information

We process the information above only to:

  • Provide the ZeManage features — protection, sync control, health monitoring, activity tracking, AI assistance, and addons — that your organization has licensed.
  • Authenticate users and authorize role-restricted actions (e.g., admin overrides, rule edits).
  • Maintain audit and compliance trails on your organization's behalf.
  • Diagnose crashes and operational issues, including journal analysis and offline-queue recovery.
  • Communicate with you about service status, security advisories, and product updates that affect your installation.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.
  • Enforce our licensing terms and detect misuse (e.g., exceeding seat counts).
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your data to train third-party AI models, and we do not run advertising in ZeManage.

Storage & Security

In Transit

All communication between the ZeManage plugin and our cloud services is encrypted with HTTPS (TLS 1.2 or higher). The real-time notification channel uses authenticated SignalR over WebSockets with TLS.

At Rest

  • Server-side data is stored in encrypted databases hosted by reputable cloud infrastructure providers.
  • Override evidence screenshots are stored in encrypted object storage with access restricted to authenticated administrators within your tenant.
  • Local files written by the plugin (auth.dat, identity.dat, the offline queue database) are encrypted with Windows DPAPI under your user account.
  • Access tokens are kept only in process memory and discarded when Revit exits.

Access Controls

  • Role-based access enforced server-side: admin-only writes require a token issued by the /admin-login endpoint carrying a profileId claim.
  • OTPs are single-use, expire after a configurable window, and are logged with the override they authorize.
  • Internal access to production data is limited to authorized engineers under documented procedures.

No system is perfectly secure. If you believe an account or device has been compromised, immediately sign out, contact your Company Administrator, and notify us at support@zestinetech.com.
